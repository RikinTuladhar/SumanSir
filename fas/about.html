About Our Club

About Us

Welcome to the Art & Recreation Club, where creativity meets adventure! Established in 2010, we've been fostering a vibrant community of artists, photographers, and outdoor enthusiasts.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive community that promotes personal growth through outdoor activities and artistic expression while fostering lasting friendships and memorable experiences.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that inspires and empowers individuals to explore their passions, embrace adventure, and develop lifelong skills.

Our Core Values

Passion

We are driven by our love for outdoor activities and artistic expression.

Community

We build strong relationships and support each other's growth.

Safety

We prioritize the well-being of our members in all activities.

Sustainability

We promote environmental responsibility in all our activities.
