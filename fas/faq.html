Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our club

What is the Art & Recreation Club?

The Art & Recreation Club is a student organization that combines creative arts with outdoor recreational activities. We provide a platform for students to explore their artistic interests while engaging in various outdoor adventures.

How can I contact the club?

You can reach us through our contact form on the website, email us at info@artrecclub.edu, or visit our office during regular hours. We're also active on social media platforms.

How do I become a member?

To become a member, simply fill out the registration form on our membership page. Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

What are the membership fees?

Our annual membership fee is $30 for students and $45 for non-students. This covers access to all regular club activities and events. Some special events may have additional costs.

What activities does the club offer?

We offer a wide range of activities including painting workshops, hiking trips, dance classes, photography sessions, camping adventures, and rock climbing. Check our activities page for a complete list.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is required for most activities. We welcome beginners and provide necessary training and guidance. Some advanced activities may require basic skills or safety certifications.

How do I register for events?

Event registration is available through our events page. Members receive early access to event registration. Simply click on the event you're interested in and follow the registration process.

What is the cancellation policy?

Events can be cancelled with full refund up to 48 hours before the start time. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee. Weather-related cancellations will be fully refunded.
