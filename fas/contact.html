Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the club?

Visit our membership page to choose a plan and fill out the registration form. Once submitted, we'll contact you within 24 hours.

What equipment do I need?

Basic equipment is provided for all activities. Members can also rent specialized equipment at discounted rates.

Are beginners welcome?

Absolutely! We welcome members of all skill levels and provide proper training and guidance for beginners.

Can I try before joining?

Yes, we offer a one-time free trial session for any activity of your choice. Contact us to schedule your trial.
